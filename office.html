Dedalus Foundation Site Launch

For Office Use Only has launched the new site for the Dedalus Foundation, founded by Abstract Expressionist painter, Robert Motherwell. Dedalus supports the public understanding of modern art and the principles of modernism that Motherwell embraced. The new site places renewed focus on Motherwell as artist and thinker, and profiles the hundreds of artists, scholars, and publications that the Foundation has supported as fellows since its inception.

LGDR

For Office Use Only has launched the new website for LGDR, an arts consortium founded by the renown New York art gallerists Dominique Lévy, Bretty Gorvy, Amalia Dayan, and Jeanne Greenberg Rohatyn.
